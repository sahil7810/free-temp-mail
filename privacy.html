Privacy Policy

Simple Summary

QuickTemp Mail is a static website that helps create a temporary email inbox using the public Mail.tm API. The site does not ask you to create a personal account and does not ask for personal information.

Mail.tm API

When you generate a temporary email, your browser sends requests to Mail.tm to create the inbox, get an access token, and fetch messages.

Browser Storage

To keep your temporary inbox available after refreshing the page, the site stores your generated temporary email, generated password, Mail.tm token, account id, refresh time, and theme choice in your browser localStorage.

Email Content

Messages are fetched from Mail.tm and shown as safe plain text where possible. This site does not run its own database for saving your emails.

Important Accounts

Do not use temporary email for important accounts such as banking, school, government services, password recovery, or long-term access.
